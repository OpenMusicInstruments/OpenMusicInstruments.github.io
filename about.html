Our Story


OMI sparked out of a desire to build cool stuff.

A group of friends with backgrounds in music and engineering, who wanted to mix both.


We started as a team in a Project Management course @The University of Porto.

The task was to build a product.


We chose to design and prototype our own instrument: B-Loop.


We got to experience how niche and how multidisciplinary this process can be and...


We loved it.


After being titled "Best Product" of that year, we decided to take it to the next level.

We wanted to share this experience with others.


OMI was born.


And it's now being incubated @UPTEC, the Science and Technology Park of University of Porto.
